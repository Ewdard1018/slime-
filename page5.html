Safety Instructions

Although we use safe materials to make slime, it is still important to avoid putting slime in your mouth.

Children need adult supervision when playing with slime. If slime touches the eyes, rinse immediately with water and seek medical help.

On our website, you can safely explore the joys of slime making. We are committed to providing safe and reliable crafting information so that you and your family can enjoy crafting together.
